discover your best

Jennifer Bolin, LICSW

counseling and therapy

I have a passion for helping others discover their best selves. Fears, anxieties, and difficult circumstances can leave us feeling stuck and hold us back from living the life we truly want.

I love joining with my clients to explore these barriers and overcome them to create a sense of peace and wholeness. I have experience working with a broad range of concerns, including anxiety, depression, trauma, disordered eating, and LGBT issues.
