Discover Your Best

Jennifer Bolin, LICSW

Counseling and Therapy

I have a passion for helping others discover their best selves. Fears, anxieties, and difficult circumstances can leave us feeling stuck and hold us back from living the life we truly want. I love joining with my clients to explore these barriers and overcome them to create peace and wholeness. I have experience working with a broad range of concerns, including anxiety, depression, trauma, disordered eating, and LGBT issues.

In my experience, therapy is the most beneficial when we engage in meaningful discussion and exploration of your thoughts, feelings, beliefs, values, and life history. I work hard to create a safe space in which there are no "taboo" topics and where authenticity and connection can grow.

I generally utilize a variety of therapeutic techniques including CBT, psychodynamics, and mindfulness. I also believe that humor is an important part of growth and healing. My goal with all of my clients is to help them arrive at a place where they feel comfortable, secure, and empowered to be themselves.

About Me

Each year I have been a therapist has solidified my certainty that this work is the best use of my skills and abilities as a person. Helping others find their best selves helps me be my best self.

I am a transplant to the Boston area; I grew up in the western US and attended graduate school at the University of Texas at Austin. I currently live in a postage-stamp-sized apartment with my wonderful partner and our somewhat neurotic, unfailingly loving mutt Harley.

In my spare time I enjoy running, crocheting, reading, and spending time with my friends. I am an avid fan of NPR with particular affinity for This American Life and Wait Wait Don't Tell Me. I am typically an extrovert, but on occasion I unplug from the world with embarrassing reality TV and Pixar movies.
